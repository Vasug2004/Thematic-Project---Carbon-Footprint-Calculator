About Us

The Carbon Footprint Tracker is designed to help individuals and business understand and manage their environental impact. This tool enables users to measure the carbon emissions from their daily activities, including travel, energy usage and dietary choices.

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platorm that educates people to make more sustainable choices. The Carbon Footprint Tracker was developed by a dedicated team whose passionate about making a difference in climate change. We have created a website and a powerpoint presentation to showcase our mission.

We use the latest technologies, including Bootstrap for the responsive design, Chart.js for data visualisation and up to date environmental data sources to ensure our calculations are accurate and meaningful.

Limitations

The calculations used in this tool based on general data that may not reflect specific individual activities. Users should consider these estimates as a starting point for understanding their carbon footprint.

Ethical Considerations

We are comitted to maintaining the privacy and security of data.
